ESG DD has moved from checkbox to material diligence workstream, driven by LP demand and EU regulation.

### Environmental

Carbon footprint (Scope 1, 2, and increasingly 3), resource usage, waste and pollution, climate risk (physical and transition).

### Social

Workforce practices, health and safety (lost-time injury rates), supply chain ethics, community impact, product safety.

### Governance

Board composition, ethics/compliance programmes, related-party transactions, tax transparency.

### ESG DD Output

Risk rating across each dimension, material risks affecting valuation or exit, an improvement plan with costs and timelines, and assessment of SFDR/CSRD reporting readiness for European portfolio companies.
