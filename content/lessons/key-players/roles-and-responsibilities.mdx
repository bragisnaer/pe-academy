Inside a PE firm, roles are clearly delineated from deal sourcing to post-close portfolio management — and understanding the career hierarchy is essential for anyone seeking to enter the industry.

The typical PE firm hierarchy runs: Analyst → Associate → Vice President → Principal/Director → Managing Director/Partner, with each level taking on progressively more responsibility for deal origination, investment committee presentations, and LP relationships.

This lesson details the day-to-day responsibilities at each level, the skills required (financial modelling, sector expertise, relationship management), how compensation and carried interest are structured across the hierarchy, and what distinguishes PE firm culture from investment banking.
