### Automated Document Review

Legal due diligence involves reviewing thousands of documents (contracts, leases, employment agreements, regulatory filings) in the data room. AI-powered document review tools (Kira Systems, Luminance, Diligence Engine) can:

- Extract key terms from contracts (change-of-control clauses, termination rights, pricing terms, exclusivity provisions) in hours rather than days
- Flag non-standard clauses that deviate from market norms
- Compare contracts against a template to identify missing protections
- Translate documents across languages (relevant for cross-border deals)

These tools do not replace legal advisers — they accelerate the review process and reduce the risk of missing a critical clause in a large data room.

### Financial Data Extraction

AI tools can extract financial data from unstructured sources (scanned PDFs, handwritten notes, legacy accounting systems) and structure it for analysis. This is particularly valuable for:
- Target companies with poor financial reporting (common in founder-led mid-market businesses)
- Bolt-on targets that lack formal management accounts
- Cross-border deals where financial records are in multiple formats and languages

### Cybersecurity Assessment Tools

Automated cybersecurity assessment platforms (BitSight, SecurityScorecard, UpGuard) provide an external view of a target company's cybersecurity posture — identifying vulnerabilities, data exposure, and compliance gaps without requiring access to the target's internal systems. PE firms increasingly run these assessments as a standard pre-DD screening step.
