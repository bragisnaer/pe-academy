Buy-and-build strategies require the LBO model to accommodate bolt-on acquisitions during the hold period. This adds significant complexity.

### Modelling a Bolt-On

**Entry assumptions for each bolt-on:**
- Acquisition year
- Bolt-on EBITDA at acquisition
- Entry multiple (typically lower than the platform's entry multiple)
- Bolt-on purchase price (EBITDA x multiple)
- Funding source (incremental debt, equity from the fund, or cash from the platform's balance sheet)
- Expected cost synergies (amount and timeline to achieve)
- Revenue synergies (if any — model conservatively)
- Integration costs (one-time expenses)

**Impact on the operating model:**
- The bolt-on's revenue and EBITDA are added to the platform's projections from the acquisition year onward
- Synergies phase in over 1–2 years (do not assume full synergies from day one)
- Integration costs are modelled as one-time expenses in the year of acquisition

**Impact on the debt schedule:**
- If funded by incremental debt, the bolt-on increases the platform's total leverage
- The incremental debt carries its own interest rate and amortisation terms
- The combined entity's leverage ratio (total debt / combined EBITDA) must remain within lender covenants

**Impact on returns:**
- The bolt-on increases the total equity invested (if funded by equity) or increases debt (if funded by debt)
- At exit, the combined entity (platform + bolt-ons + synergies) is valued at the platform's exit multiple — this is where multiple arbitrage creates value
- MOIC = Exit equity value / Total sponsor equity invested (platform equity + bolt-on equity contributions)

### Multiple Arbitrage: A Worked Example

**Platform:** Acquired at 10x EBITDA. EBITDA at entry: €30M. EV: €300M.

**Bolt-on:** Acquired in Year 2 at 6x EBITDA. Bolt-on EBITDA: €5M. Bolt-on cost: €30M. Funded with incremental debt.

**Synergies:** €2M annual cost synergies, fully realised by Year 3.

**At exit (Year 5):**
- Combined EBITDA: €30M (platform, grown to €38M at 5% CAGR) + €5M (bolt-on, grown to €6.4M) + €2M synergies = **€46.4M**
- Exit multiple: 11x (modest expansion from 10x)
- Exit EV: €46.4M x 11 = **€510.4M**

**Value from multiple arbitrage alone:**
The bolt-on was acquired at 6x (€30M for €5M EBITDA). At exit it is valued at 11x. The re-rating on the bolt-on's Year 5 EBITDA (€6.4M): (11 – 6) x €6.4M = **€32M of value created purely from the multiple differential.**

This is the economic engine of buy-and-build.
