### Artificial Intelligence in Portfolio Companies

AI is no longer a future technology — it is being deployed in PE portfolio companies today. Common applications:
- Revenue operations (AI-driven pricing, demand forecasting, customer segmentation)
- Back-office automation (accounts payable/receivable, compliance monitoring, reporting)
- Customer service (chatbots, intelligent routing, sentiment analysis)
- Manufacturing (predictive maintenance, quality control, supply chain optimisation)

PE firms that can deploy AI capabilities across their portfolio companies have a potential value creation edge. Several firms have established dedicated AI/technology teams to identify and implement AI use cases across portfolios.

### Climate Transition Investing

The global transition to a lower-carbon economy creates PE investment opportunities in:
- Renewable energy infrastructure (solar, wind, battery storage)
- Energy efficiency services and technologies
- Electric vehicle supply chain
- Circular economy businesses (recycling, remanufacturing, waste reduction)
- Carbon capture and storage

Several PE firms have launched dedicated climate or energy transition funds, and ESG-oriented LPs are increasing allocations to climate-related strategies.

### Wealth Transfer and Family Business Succession

An estimated $15 trillion in business assets will transfer between generations over the next two decades, concentrated in family-owned mid-market businesses across North America, Europe, and Asia. Many of these businesses will be sold to PE because the next generation does not want to (or cannot) run them. This demographic wave represents a structural tailwind for mid-market PE deal sourcing.

### Key Terms Introduced in This Supplement

- **Pre-mortem** — a structured exercise asking "what went wrong?" before committing to a deal, to identify and mitigate failure modes
- **Kill discipline** — the willingness to walk away from deals despite sunk costs and competitive pressure
- **Commitment pacing** — the LP's strategy for committing to PE funds at a rate that maintains target allocation levels
- **Climate transition investing** — PE investment in businesses and infrastructure related to the shift toward a lower-carbon economy
